Why go solar?

The demand for renewable energy solutions is increasing, driven by environmental concerns and government incentives for solar energy adoption. The U.S. Government has a very aggressive plan for the country which involves a 50% electric vehicle adoption by the year 2030, however the infrastructure cannot support this number of electric vehicles, thereby creating a greater demand for affordable home energy.
